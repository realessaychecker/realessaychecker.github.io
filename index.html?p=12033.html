Some effective phrases for conclusion in IELTS essay and PTE writing

Posted on 04/11/2015 REC TeamPosted in Phrase

Here we have collected useful phrases for begining of a cncolusion part in IELTS writing module – task 2. They are also helpful for PTE writing:

Writing a conclusion; phrases (2)

Following phrases can be useful in a conclusion.
Taking everything into consideration
Clearly
To conclude
Ultimately
In conclusion
Examples:
Ultimately the discovery may lead to the development of new contraceptives.
In conclusion, I think Hamlet is a great play.
Taking everything into consideration, we're probably better off now.
