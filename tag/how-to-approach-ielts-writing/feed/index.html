Useful Vocabulary for IELTS

laconic     ADJ. using only a few words to say sth; brief to the point. OPP: verbose
Examples:
She had a laconic wit.
the sportscaster’s color commentary tends to be laconic but very much to the point.
laconically ADV.
‘she left’, said Sarah laconically.
