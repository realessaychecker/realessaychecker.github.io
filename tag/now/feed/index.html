Learn new vocabulary for IELTS

mitigate verb. alleviate; lessen in intensity; moderate; appease.
Examples:
Because solar energy has the power to reduce green-house gases and provide increased energy efficiency, conversion to the use of solar energy may help mitigate global warming.
We have to figure out a way to mitigate the costs.
It is unclear how to mitigate the effects of tourism on the island.
