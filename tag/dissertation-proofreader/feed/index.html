Lesson (2)

How to write? /Task (1)
Describing data

After selecting the key features in IELTS task (1) essay you should start to describe them. We suggest you to use the “passive structure” while describing the data.

Consider the above pie chart, bar chart and diagram and write one or two short statements describing your data. You can use the following verbs:

Show/Demonstrate/ Indicate/ represents/ draw conclusion from

For example, the following sentences describe the important points of the pie chart, bar chart and diagram, respectively.

1- It is demonstrated that around 50% of the proportion of the pie chart is allocated to the roads.

2-According to the bar chart, it is shown that 100% of the girls and boys aged between 5 and 15 years old watch TV or videos.

3- It is indicated in the diagram that during 1960-1980 Japan had the lowest proportion of population aged 65 and over that it was around 3%.
