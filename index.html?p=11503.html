Get your PTE and IELTS Writing assessed by our experienced team. We also give you an estimate for your PTE and IELTS score.

1 on 1 IELTS and PTE English classes

Join our 1 on 1 class to achieve your IELTS and PTE goals! You are supervised by our experienced teachers while practising each IELTS and PTE module. Each class includes the following items:

  • One (1) essay for each writing Task is proofread and edited
  • Active writing (student and teacher will write a complete writing step by step together)
  • Active reading (student and teacher will go through reading questions together)
  • Four (4) IELTS mock exams before the real IELTS exam.
  • The classes are held in Sydney CBD. We have also an option to run your class Online.
